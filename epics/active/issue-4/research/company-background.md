# Virtual Inc. Company Background and Profile

## Executive Summary

Virtual Inc. is a leading professional services firm specializing in association management for technology consortia, standards organizations, and professional societies. Founded around 1999-2000, the company has spent 25 years building expertise in helping membership-based organizations achieve their strategic goals and make a lasting impact on global technology standards and innovation.

## Company Overview

**Company Name:** Virtual, Inc.  
**Founded:** Circa 1999-2000 (25+ years in operation)  
**Headquarters:** Wakefield, Massachusetts, United States  
**Region:** Greater Boston Area, East Coast, New England  
**Industry:** Professional Services, Association Management  
**CEO:** Andy Freed  
**LinkedIn Followers:** 4,713+  

## Mission and Vision

**Mission:** To help membership-based organizations achieve sustainable success and make their mark on the world through strategic advisory and execution services.

**Core Purpose:** Virtual enables organizations to achieve their goals by providing comprehensive management solutions that drive real results for technology consortia, standards groups, and associations that are forming, growing, or changing.
